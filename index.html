About Debbie's Den

Quite a few items get crocheted here at Debbie's Den. Doilies, baby clothes, and custom hats to name a few. But we specialize in handmade bouquets of crocheted flowers.

We think giving that special someone a bouquet of all their favorite flowers, a bouquet that will stay just the way you gave it, is quite a special gift. Over thirty years of crocheting experience is put into hand-making each and every flower.

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide quality, custom, handcrafted products to people who appreciate items made just for them. Receiving a gift made specially for you is a wonderful feeling.


VISION: Our products aren't for everyone. Far from just another stock greeting card on a shelf, or a standard bouquet from a big-box store, our products aren't made in a factory half the world away. They're made entirely by hand, in a tiny town in Northern Michigan.
