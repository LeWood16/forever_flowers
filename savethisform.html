About us

Quite a few items get crocheted here at Forever Flowers. Doilies, baby clothes, and custom hats to name a few. But we specialize in handmade bouquets of crocheted flowers.

We do mean forever! We think that giving that special someone a bouquet of all their favorite flowers, a bouquet that they can keep forever, is quite a special gift. Over thirty years of crocheting experience is put into making each and every flower, all made by hand.
